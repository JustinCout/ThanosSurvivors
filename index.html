THANOS'

SURVIVORS

At the end of Avengers: Infinity War, Thanos won. He collected all of the infinity stones, granting him the omnipotent powers that can alter the universe. Following the struggle to collect the final stone, the mind stone, Thanos places the final stone in his gauntlet and snaps his finger. The impact of a single snap was so devastating, it wiped out 50% of all living things from existence.
